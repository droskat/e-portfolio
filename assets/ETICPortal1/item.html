About This Project

The ETIC Web Portal is a platform for uploading and showcasing ongoing and finished projects. Proposals for projects can also be uploaded by organisations that wish to develop their projects by students. The portal also features a learning studio that includes workshops,courses and seminars that students can wish to take part in.

Project Data

THE GOAL

To provide a platform for learning courses of choice and accessing ongoing and finished projects in the university.



THE RESULT

To deliver a web portal that students as well as organisations can access according to their use.

THE IDEA

The choice of courses to students is very limited. Often they do not get to learn what they want. This project will provide a platform to students to learn courses of their choice.


FEATURES

The portal features an incubation unit that includes a project database and a learning studio where online courses,workshops and seminars are stored.
